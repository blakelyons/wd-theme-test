Swimmers may only register for one option. There will be a maximum of four swimmers per lane. With smaller groups, coaches are also be able to provide each swimmer more individualized attention.

BRONZE Requirements (Grade 2nd - 5th) 
Participants must be able to swim the length of the pool (25 meters) in a time less than 1 minute.  Participants will be introduced to all four strokes as well as proper turns and starts.

SILVER Requirements (Grade 6th - 8th)
Participants should be able to swim a continuous 50 meters of each stroke, with their time to complete 50 meters of Freestyle under 1:15. Proper technique will be emphasized as well as endurance.

Gold Requirements (Grade 9th - 12th) 
Participants should be able to swim 10 x 100 Free on a 2:00 interval.  This class is for high school swimmers preparing for competition. 

Coaches reserve the right to move swimmers up and/or down to provide the best challenge for each swimmer's ability. 

Attendees must wear a mask upon entry and in common spaces, sanitize hands, practice physical distancing, and be prepared to have their temperature taken. Masks will not be required during the class.  Complete protocols will be emailed once registered. For a complete list of our health and safety protocols, visit our Resumption of Services page.
